Welcome to chess 101!

here you can gain chess knowledge in order to be able to play a full chess game, you might be wondering though, why learn chess anyway?
To begin with ,below you can see some of the benefits of systematic chess study:
  • Develops perspective.
  • Improves memory.
  • Deepens focus.
  • Elevates creativity.
  • Boosts planning skills.
  • Increases self-awareness.
    Now before you get dissapointed , chess is hard and requires investing a good amount of time in order to consider yourself a mid-range player, keep in mind that you will make a lot of mistakes along the way which is expected. Those disadvantages however do not outweigh the advantages and thats why chess is so popular, the best way to learn chess is to see it as a challenge or a riddle.
